I'm looking for my tickets

Lost your tickets, or haven't received them? No problem, we can help you find them.

Sometimes emails from Eventix can't find their way into your mailbox because of spam-filters. Using the tool below you can retrieve your tickets online.

Login to your bank account and look at the payment to Eventix on your bank statement. In the description of the payment you will find a 16-character Payment ID consisting of letters and numbers. Fill in the Payment ID below, to download your tickets.
