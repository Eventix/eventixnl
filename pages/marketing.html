Feature Marketing tools

Get deeper visitor insights by using the marketing tools of the biggest brands.

At Eventix we have many features all designed to make your daily life and selling tickets easier. So how do we do it?

  • Pixels
    We have two pixels; for Facebook and LinkedIn, which does the same beautiful thing. Pixels show you which visitor originates from which link. Tracking links is useful for showing you what social media post works best, which visitors you should retarget and other ways to benefit you.


  • Google Analytics & Google Adwords
    With Google, there are many neat tricks that we can apply to your event.

    Google Analytics gives you a clear overview of what kind of individuals browse your website. For example, Google Analytics will give you information about the demographics, interests and behaviour of your target group.

    With Google Adwords, you can advertise in the Google search engine making you the top search while also advertising on Google Adwords supported websites. Here potential customers are targeted through the words they search.


  • Coupons
    What about a share and like contest? We have a function where you can customise your coupons to give a discount to those special people.
