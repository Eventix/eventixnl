Seamless integrations

Eventix integrates easily with any platform. Connect with preset plugins of industry leading web services or build on top of our open API. To read more about building custom integrations check out our API documentation .

TicketSwap

We counter ticketing fraud in secondary markets for all our tickets sold through our Secure Swap integration with TicketSwap. They check the validity for every Eventix ticket sold via TicketSwap. Fake tickets or tickets marked as "non-transferable" cannot be offered. The original ticket is replaced by a ticket with a unique code and personal details of the secondhand buyer.

MailChimp

Stay in touch with your visitors. Connecting MailChimp keeps your mailing lists up to date, resulting in more efficient MailChimp campaigns. Send out invites for a registration page or set up mailing lists.

Google Tag Manager

Let us help you with the most advanced marketing features! Google Tag Manager gives you a clear overview of what happens online. This way you get to know your target group in a unique and personal way. Monitor detailed customer behaviour and connect this information for retargeting and tracking.

Android and iOS

We offer a mobile-first experience and are highly enthusiastic about the integrations of our ticket shop in mobile apps. Implement the ticket shop in your Android and iOS app. Offer your user the smoothest purchasing experience while you watch your sales increase.

Custom Code

We’re giving you access to our API, meaning you can build whatever you want! The Eventix Ticket service is built entirely on top of the Eventix ticketing API. We have already seen a lot of great use-cases such as integrations with Administration Software, CRM platforms, ERP systems, such as MS Dynamics and SAP.

The possibilities are limitless. Let us know if you need help building an integration. We are happy to lend you a hand.

Step 1 Create your event within minutes

Realise the full potential of your event by getting started.

Create your event in the Eventix dashboard or import it directly from Facebook. You will be able to customise your event to the liking of your visitors entirely. The experience you provide will start at the creation of your event itself.

Step 2 Sell your tickets on- and offline

A fully customisable and user-friendly ticketshop. Try it out yourself!

When visitors order tickets through your ticketshop, they can do so within a few taps on their smartphones. Your shop can be customised in many ways, including a custom design, adding your Facebook like button and any number of unique tickets.

Step 3 Easy scanning makes fast queues

Scan up to a thousand tickets per scanner, per hour!

With our Eventix app, available for Android and iOS, every smartphone can become a scanner, therefore you will never run out of devices to scan with. The scan-app is straightforward, easy to use and features full customer support.


Step 4 Use data to boost your sales

Get to know your audience and discover what they love.

Your ticket sales generates a lot of data. This data gives keen insights for marketing, sales and strengthens the personal bond between you and your visitors. Measure conversions and track the return on your marketing investment so you can improve your strategy. Many possibilities await, let your event grow.
