Seamless integrations

Eventix’s open structure allows to integrate easily with any platform. Connect using preset plugins of industry leading web services, or build on top of our open API. To read more about building custom integrations check out our API documentation .

    • TicketSwap
    • Our Secure Swap integration with TicketSwap counters ticketing fraud in secondary markets for all Eventix events. For every ticket sold on TicketSwap, the validity is checked in our servers. Tickets that are fake, or marked as "non-transferable" are not able to be offered. If a sale is possible the originally sold ticket is replaced by a new ticket with a unique code, and the secondhand buyer’s personal details on the ticket.
    • Learn more about TicketSwap
    • MailChimp
    • Connected MailChimp lists keep your mailing lists up to date, resulting in more effective MailChimp campaigns. Send out invites for a registration page, or set up mailing lists to stay in sync with your events ticket-buyers.
    • Learn more about MailChimp
    • Google Tag Manager
    • Connect your Google Tag Manager account to fire custom tags from within the ticketshop. This allows for tracking detailed customer behaviour and connecting this information directly with your Facebook ads, and AdWords accounts for retargeting and tracking. We’ll even help you get started with these advanced marketing features!
    • Get started with GTM
    • Android and iOS
    • The Eventix ticketshop is tailored for a mobile-first experience. That’s why we are highly enthusiastic about integrations of our ticketshop in native Android and iOS Apps. Implement the ticketshop within your event apps, and watch your sales increase!
    • Read more
    • Custom Code
    • The Eventix Ticketservice is built completely on top of the Eventix ticketing API. The beauty is the we’re giving you access to our API, meaning you can build whatever we can! We’ve already seen a lot of great use-cases such as integrations with Administration Software, CRM platforms, ERP systems, such as MS Dynamics and SAP. The possibilities are limitless. Let us know if you need help building an integration, and we’ll be happy to lend you a hand.
    • Read more about our API
